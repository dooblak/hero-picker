Though the travelers and road trippers who used to cross the US on historic 
Route 66 are gone, the Main Street of America still stands, a testament to a 
simpler time. The gas stations, roadside shops, and cafes have gone into disuse, 
and the fabled Deadlock Gorge is mostly seen from the comfort of transcontinental 
train cars. But amid the fading monuments of that earlier era, the outlaws of the 
Deadlock Gang are planning their biggest heist yet.
